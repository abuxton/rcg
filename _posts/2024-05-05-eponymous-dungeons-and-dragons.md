Do you play TTRPGs or do you Play D&D is D&D just fantasy or is Call of Cthulhu simply D&D with horror tropes, Starfinder sci-fi D&D. Has the hobby of Table Top Roleplaying become ubiquitous with Dungeons and Dragons despite WotC attempts under Hasbro to profit from D&D, have they really just succeeded in making the name universal for aspects of Table Top Roleplaying games are we now all just playing a game of Dungeons and Dragons regardless of rules, setting, aspect or publisher. Has D&D or Dungeons and Dragons outgrown it’s creators and owners to become eponymous(<https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/eponymous>) for role-playing games.
